About me

Hello,

My name is Diana Sanderson. I am currently a Notice Processing Officer with Sheffield City Council. I process applications, log/process reports relating to parking, Enforcement as well as Events as well as manage/ process Representations & Formal Appeals within the Transport Services. In my ever-changing role I am regularly exposed to new systems, process and guidelines. I am required to research, adapt and learn new skills to fulfill my duties. I also interpret maps, plans and Traffic orders for the purposes of answering inquiries in relation to parking matters over the phone to the public, businesses and all citizens of Sheffield as well as other counties and internal colleagues; face to face, over the phone or via written form. I understand process, legislation and guidelines are changing all the time and even in the smallest of changes; massive steps may need to be taken.

I also have experience with Customer Services in different areas within Sheffield City Council - I believe I pick up new skills quickly and thrive when given challenges and this is why I have been given plenty of opportunities to expand my set of skills / knowledge and progress within Sheffield City Council.

I believe my previous experience in Customer Services as well as my Processing/ Back office skills has given me the various transferable skills to build my career in the Tech world. I also believe the 6 months I have had on secondment from my role to participate in the development/re-build of the Sheffield City Council’s website has given me an opportunity to grow and learn alongside Senior developers, creating clean code and following agile practices. Although the experience was short it encourages me to move, to learn and progres further, working with freelance on Pro Bono projects & small local business projects.

I am deeply passionate about customer service, technology and the creative arts, it simultaneously challenges me, intrigues me and motivates me. My ideal day would be a day that gives me an opportunity to utilize my passions; to create, inspire and grow.

Please find below; my relevant skills, attributes and qualifications. I look forward to an opportunity to reveal my enthusiasm for a chance to grow along side you reaching your goals and surpassing mine.
